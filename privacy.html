Privacy Policy for PhotoEasy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

PhotoEasy Studio ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information PhotoEasy handles and why. PhotoEasy is local-first: standard editing happens entirely on your device, and information only leaves your device for the specific optional features described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your images and edits: All standard editing and on-device AI tools (e.g. background removal, erase, upscale) are performed locally on your device and are never uploaded.
  • Cloud AI features (optional): When you use a cloud-powered feature — Generative Fill, Vary Image, or Generate — the relevant image or selected region and your text prompt are sent over an encrypted connection to our backend and to a third-party AI image provider in order to produce the result. We do not use your images or prompts to train models, and we do not retain them beyond what is needed to deliver and account for the generation.
  • Account & purchases: Cloud AI requires signing in with your Microsoft account. We receive a Microsoft-provided identifier and your account email to create your account, track your free-trial allowance, and apply any credits you purchase through the Microsoft Store. Payments are handled by Microsoft; we do not receive your payment details.
  • Anonymous usage & diagnostics: To understand how the app is used and to fix problems, PhotoEasy may send a small amount of anonymous data — basic feature-usage events (such as opening an image, running an AI action, or exporting), the app version, and anonymous crash/error reports (error type, message, and a technical stack trace). This data is tied only to a random, anonymous device identifier; it contains no personal information and no image or document content.

2. Network Usage

PhotoEasy uses an internet connection for:

  • Model downloading: On first use of certain on-device AI features, the app may download model files from trusted repositories (such as GitHub or HuggingFace) to enable local processing.
  • Cloud AI: Sending images/prompts to our backend and the AI provider when you use a cloud AI feature, and signing in / applying purchases as described above.
  • Anonymous usage & diagnostics: Sending the anonymous events and crash reports described in Section 1.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use advertising or third-party tracking SDKs. We rely on the following service providers solely to deliver the features above:

  • Microsoft — account sign-in and Microsoft Store purchases.
  • Our AI image provider — processes cloud AI requests to generate images. Images and prompts sent for a cloud generation are processed by this provider to return your result.

4. Data Security & Retention

Data in transit is protected with encryption (HTTPS). Your images never leave your device unless you use a cloud AI feature, and your local files always remain on your device. We retain only the minimal account and billing records needed to operate the service and the anonymous usage/diagnostic data described above.

5. Children's Privacy

PhotoEasy is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or need support, please open an issue at: github.com/Chris533/photoeasy-site/issues
