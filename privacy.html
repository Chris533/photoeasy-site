Privacy Policy for PhotoEasy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

PhotoEasy Studio ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how PhotoEasy handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

PhotoEasy is designed to be a privacy-first application.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personal identification information (such as name, email address, or phone number).
  • Image Data: All image processing and AI-powered edits in the desktop editor are performed locally on your device. We do not upload your images to any servers.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your usage of the application.

2. Network Usage

PhotoEasy requires an internet connection for the following purpose:

  • Model Downloading: On the first run, or when a specific AI feature is used for the first time, the application may download necessary AI model files from trusted third-party repositories (such as GitHub or HuggingFace). These downloads are used solely to enable local processing features.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, there is no risk of your personal data being breached from our systems. Your images remain on your local storage at all times.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or need support, please open an issue at: github.com/Chris533/photoeasy-site/issues
