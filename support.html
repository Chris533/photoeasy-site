Need help with PhotoEasy? You're in the right place. The fastest way to reach us is to open an issue — we read every one and usually reply within a couple of days.

Contact us

  • Support & bug reports: github.com/Chris533/photoeasy-site/issues — open a new issue describing the problem and your Windows version.
  • In the app: use the Feedback option in the top menu to send us a note.

When reporting a problem, it helps to include: what you were doing, your Windows version (10 or 11), and a screenshot if the issue is visual.

Frequently asked questions

How do I generate an image from a prompt?

Open PhotoEasy and, on the home screen, type a description in the prompt box, pick a size (square, landscape, or portrait), and select Generate. You can also choose New with AI from the top menu at any time.

What's the difference between the on-device tools and the cloud AI?

Background Removal, Smart Erase, and Upscale run locally on your device — they work offline and your images are never uploaded. Image generation, Generative Fill, Vary Image, and Outpaint are cloud-powered: the image and prompt are sent securely to our service to produce the result. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Do I need an account, and what does it cost?

On-device editing is free and needs no account. Cloud AI features require signing in with your Microsoft account and include a limited free trial; after that you can buy credits through the Microsoft Store. Payments are handled by Microsoft.

My free generations or credits aren't updating.

Make sure you're signed in and connected to the internet, then reopen the AI panel — the remaining allowance refreshes after each generation. If it still looks wrong, sign out and back in, or contact us at the link above.

The first AI edit is downloading something — is that normal?

Yes. The first time you use an on-device AI tool, PhotoEasy downloads the model file so it can run locally afterward. This happens once per tool and needs an internet connection.

Which file formats can I export?

PhotoEasy exports PNG, PNG-8, and JPEG. Background removal produces transparent PNGs.

What are the system requirements?

Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit). Cloud AI features additionally require an internet connection and a Microsoft account.
